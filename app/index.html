Giving a precious Gift...

Donating eggs/ova enables many people who cannot conceive naturally to have children. Eggs donated through Oocyt are used in fertility procedures (IVF), not research and may result in conception.

Donation of Eggs/ova will not affect the donor’s own fertility, health or future chances of her own pregnancy. Not all of a donor’s ova are removed during donation. A woman is born with all her eggs and every month the body releases a few of these eggs. Hormonal stimulation will allow the eggs that would have been dormant to grow and be released in a cycle. Many infertile couples have been able to have children through use of donated eggs. It is a precious gift.

Who can become an Oocyt Donor?

The decision to become an egg donor with oöcyt is not one to be taken lightly. Guidelines are strict in order to ensure the safety of our donors and our prospective egg recipients. Although the process may require time and dedication, the reward for helping a family is immeasurable.

Qualification Criteria

  • Women between the ages of 21 to 27 with good overall health
  • Body mass index (BMI) between 18 to 26kg/m2
  • Non-smoker
  • Live or work within 1 hour of one of our registered centers
  • Graduated from high school
  • Undergraduate

How to become an egg donor?

For our prospective egg donors, we've made the application process as simple as possible, while keeping it thorough and robust enough to accurately assess whether or not becoming an egg donor is right for you.

Apply

Fill out our pre-screening form for us to determine if you are a potential candidate. If you are, we will be in touch so you can complete the rest of the application forms

Be Screened

Our donor coordinator will call you to schedule your first visit with. You will undergo a series of blood tests and consultations. This step can take up to three weeks. Please feel free to read more about our donor process.

Complete the Retrieval process

You will be required to take injections to make your ovaries produce the eggs. Our donor team will be with you every step of the way to make sure you are comfortable and committed to the program. Monitoring visits are required to make sure you are responding to the medication. This step may take an additional 2 weeks
